# Fourth Platform Packages

## Package Comparison

Fourth offers three platform packages designed to match different stages of operational maturity. With the **Restaurant Operations Suite** (launched April 2025), all packages now include access to the unified platform with module-level differentiation.

### Essentials
**Best for:** Single-location or small multi-unit operators (2-20 locations) getting started with digital workforce management.

**Core value:** Replace paper schedules and manual time tracking with a simple, mobile-first solution.

**Typical customer:** Independent restaurant group, small franchise operator.

**Starting point:** Many customers begin with Essentials and upgrade as they grow. The HotSchedules mobile experience drives rapid adoption.

### Professional
**Best for:** Growing multi-unit operators (20-200 locations) who need scheduling optimization and compliance automation.

**Core value:** Reduce labor costs **2-4%** through AI-optimized scheduling while maintaining compliance across multiple jurisdictions.

**Typical customer:** Regional restaurant chain, mid-market franchise group, multi-brand operator.

**Key upgrade triggers:** Growing past 20 locations, entering new states with complex labor laws, need for compliance automation.

### Enterprise
**Best for:** Large multi-unit operators (200+ locations) who need a complete workforce management platform.

**Core value:** End-to-end platform that replaces 5-7 point solutions, providing a single source of truth for the entire employee lifecycle from hire to retire.

**Typical customer:** National QSR chain, large casual dining group, hospitality company.

**What makes Enterprise different:**
- **Fourth iQ** AI engine with demand forecasting and predictive analytics
- **Fourth Payroll** with hospitality-specific calculations (tips, split shifts, multi-state)
- **MacromatiX** inventory and procurement
- **PeopleMatter** applicant tracking and onboarding
- **Fuego** earned wage access ($0 employer cost)
- **Dedicated CSM** with quarterly business reviews
- **Full API access** for custom integrations and BI tools

### Restaurant Operations Suite Tiers

With the April 2025 launch, Fourth introduced bundled suite pricing:

| Suite | Includes | Best For |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **Workforce Suite** | HotSchedules + Compliance + Payroll | Operators focused on labor optimization |
| **Operations Suite** | Workforce Suite + MacromatiX + Fourth iQ | Operators wanting unified labor + inventory |
| **Complete Suite** | Operations Suite + PeopleMatter + Fuego + Full Analytics | Full platform, maximum value |

Suite pricing offers **15-25% savings** compared to purchasing modules individually.
