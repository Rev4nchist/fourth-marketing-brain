# Fourth Enterprise Platform Playbook

## Platform Overview

**The Fourth Platform connects every part of a restaurant's operation - people, product, and profit - into one coordinated system that drives performance. It gives operators a single place to plan, forecast, and act using AI automation that turns data into smarter, faster decisions.**

The Fourth Platform is how leading restaurant brands connect operations to outcomes. It unifies Workforce Management and Inventory Management within one coordinated system powered by Fourth 360, the data and automation fabric that connects every part of the business. Within each pillar, Fourth iQ serves as the AI engine that forecasts demand, recommends actions, and guides managers in real time through the iQ App. Underneath it all, the Platform Foundation provides enterprise-grade reliability, integrations, and security that keep data flowing across systems. Finally, the platform is reinforced by a Services and Support layer that drives adoption, ROI, and continuous improvement. Together, these components form a connected ecosystem that turns every insight into action and every action into measurable results.

### Value Propositions

- **Operate as one business: Replaces disconnected systems with a unified platform that connects labor, inventory, and financial workflows for total visibility and control.**
- **Turn data into decisions: Uses AI forecasting and guided recommendations from Fourth iQ to help operators act faster and stay ahead of demand.**
- **Unlock measurable results: Helps restaurants reduce costs, increase accuracy, and improve margins across every location.**
- **Drive long-term value: Backed by expert services that accelerate rollout, adoption, and sustained performance improvements.**
