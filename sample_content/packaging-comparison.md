# Fourth Platform Packages

## Package Comparison

Fourth offers three platform packages designed to match different stages of operational maturity.

### Package Overview

| Feature | Essentials | Professional | Enterprise |
|---------|-----------|-------------|------------|
| **Scheduling** | Basic scheduling | AI-optimized scheduling | AI + demand forecasting |
| **Time & Attendance** | Clock-in/out, basic rules | Geofencing, break compliance | Full compliance engine |
| **Manager App** | View schedules | Edit + approve | Full operational dashboard |
| **Team Member App** | View schedule, swap shifts | + Availability, open shifts | + Earned wage access |
| **Reporting** | 10 standard reports | 25+ reports + custom | Unlimited + analytics |
| **Integrations** | 1 POS integration | Up to 3 integrations | Unlimited integrations |
| **Payroll** | - | Add-on | Included |
| **Applicant Tracking** | - | Add-on | Included |
| **Onboarding** | - | - | Included |
| **Demand Forecasting** | - | - | AI-powered |
| **Labor Budgeting** | - | - | Included |
| **API Access** | - | Read-only | Full read/write |
| **Support** | Email (business hours) | Email + phone | Dedicated CSM + priority |
| **Implementation** | Self-service | Guided (4-6 weeks) | White-glove (8-12 weeks) |

### Essentials
**Best for:** Single-location or small multi-unit operators (2-20 locations) getting started with digital workforce management.

**Core value:** Replace paper schedules and manual time tracking with a simple, mobile-first solution.

**Typical customer:** Independent restaurant group, small franchise operator.

### Professional
**Best for:** Growing multi-unit operators (20-200 locations) who need scheduling optimization and compliance automation.

**Core value:** Reduce labor costs 2-4% through AI-optimized scheduling while maintaining compliance across multiple jurisdictions.

**Typical customer:** Regional restaurant chain, mid-market franchise group, multi-brand operator.

### Enterprise
**Best for:** Large multi-unit operators (200+ locations) who need a complete workforce management platform.

**Core value:** End-to-end platform that replaces 5-7 point solutions, providing a single source of truth for the entire employee lifecycle from hire to retire.

**Typical customer:** National QSR chain, large casual dining group, hospitality company.
