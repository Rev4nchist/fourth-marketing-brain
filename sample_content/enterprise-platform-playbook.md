# Fourth Enterprise Platform Playbook

## Platform Overview

Fourth is the leading end-to-end restaurant and hospitality workforce management platform. Our cloud-native solution connects every aspect of workforce operations - from hiring and onboarding through scheduling, time & attendance, payroll, and performance management.

### Architecture

Fourth's platform is built on a modern microservices architecture deployed on Microsoft Azure:

- **Cloud-Native**: 99.9% uptime SLA, auto-scaling, multi-region deployment
- **API-First**: RESTful APIs enable integration with 200+ POS, payroll, and HR systems
- **Single Data Model**: Unified employee record across all modules eliminates data silos
- **Mobile-First**: Native iOS and Android apps for managers and team members

### The Five Pillars

1. **Hire & Onboard** - Applicant tracking, digital onboarding, I-9/E-Verify, document management
2. **Schedule & Optimize** - AI-powered demand forecasting, auto-scheduling, shift marketplace
3. **Time & Attendance** - Biometric clock-in, geofencing, break compliance, tip management
4. **Pay & Comply** - Multi-state payroll, tax filing, ACA compliance, tip credit calculations
5. **Engage & Retain** - Performance reviews, pulse surveys, recognition, career pathing

### Platform Differentiators

- **Purpose-Built for Hospitality**: Unlike generic HCM platforms (Workday, ADP), Fourth is built specifically for the complexities of restaurant and hospitality workforce management - tip calculations, split shifts, minor labor laws, seasonal hiring spikes.
- **Demand-Driven Scheduling**: Our AI scheduling engine uses historical sales data, weather, events, and seasonal patterns to forecast labor demand with 95%+ accuracy.
- **Real-Time Labor Analytics**: Managers see labor cost as a percentage of revenue in real-time, enabling in-shift adjustments.
- **Compliance Engine**: Automated enforcement of federal, state, and local labor laws including predictive scheduling ordinances, overtime rules, and break requirements across 50 states.
- **Scale**: Processes payroll for 400,000+ employees monthly. Manages scheduling for 20,000+ locations.

## Target Market

### Primary: Multi-Unit Restaurant Operators
- QSR (Quick Service) chains: 50-5,000+ locations
- Fast Casual: 20-500 locations
- Casual Dining: 20-500 locations
- Fine Dining groups: 5-50 locations

### Secondary: Hospitality & Adjacent
- Hotels and resorts
- Stadium and event venues
- Contract food service (Aramark, Sodexo, Compass)
- Senior living facilities with food service operations

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- 50+ locations (sweet spot: 200-2,000)
- $50M+ annual revenue
- Hourly workforce >70% of employees
- Multiple states/jurisdictions
- Current pain: fragmented point solutions or legacy on-premise system
