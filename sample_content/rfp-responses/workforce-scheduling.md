# RFP Response: Workforce Scheduling Capabilities

## Standard RFP Answers - Scheduling Module

### Q: Describe your workforce scheduling capabilities.
**Confidence: GROUNDED**

Fourth provides an AI-powered workforce scheduling platform purpose-built for the restaurant and hospitality industry. Our scheduling engine combines demand forecasting, labor budgeting, compliance rules, and employee preferences to generate optimized schedules automatically.

Key capabilities include:
- **AI Auto-Scheduling**: Generates compliant, cost-optimized schedules in minutes based on forecasted demand, labor budget targets, employee skills, certifications, availability, and preferences.
- **Demand Forecasting**: Machine learning models analyze 2+ years of historical sales data, weather, local events, and seasonal patterns to predict labor demand with 95%+ accuracy at 15-minute intervals.
- **Shift Marketplace**: Enables team members to swap, pick up, or drop shifts through a mobile app with manager approval rules, ensuring coverage while reducing manager workload.
- **Compliance Engine**: Automatically enforces federal, state, and local scheduling laws including predictive scheduling ordinances (San Francisco, Oregon, NYC, Chicago, etc.), minor labor restrictions, overtime rules, and required break periods.
- **Labor Cost Optimization**: Real-time labor-to-sales ratio tracking enables managers to make in-shift adjustments. Typical customers see 2-4% labor cost reduction within 90 days.

### Q: How does your system handle multi-location scheduling?
**Confidence: GROUNDED**

Fourth supports enterprise multi-location scheduling with:
- Centralized schedule templates that can be customized per location
- Cross-location employee sharing (floating staff pools)
- District/region-level oversight with roll-up reporting
- Location-specific compliance rule sets (handles multiple state/city ordinances)
- Bulk schedule operations across location groups
- Role-based access: Corporate sets guardrails, GMs execute within them

### Q: What scheduling compliance features are included?
**Confidence: GROUNDED**

Fourth's compliance engine covers:
- **Predictive Scheduling**: Advance notice requirements (7-14 days), right to rest, schedule change premiums, good faith estimates
- **Overtime**: Federal, state, and local overtime rules including daily overtime (CA), 7th consecutive day, and weighted average overtime
- **Minor Labor Laws**: Age-specific hour restrictions, prohibited task enforcement, school-day vs. non-school-day rules by state
- **Break Requirements**: Automatic meal and rest break scheduling per state law, break attestation tracking
- **Consecutive Day Limits**: Maximum consecutive days worked per jurisdiction
- **Clopening Prevention**: Minimum hours between closing and opening shifts

All compliance rules are maintained by Fourth's legal/compliance team and updated automatically when laws change.

**Confidence: GROUNDED**

Typical implementation timelines:

### Q: Describe your mobile capabilities for scheduling.
**Confidence: GROUNDED**

Fourth offers native mobile apps for both iOS and Android:

**Manager App:**
- View, edit, and publish schedules
- Approve/deny shift swaps and time-off requests
- Real-time labor cost vs. budget dashboard
- Push notifications for coverage gaps and compliance warnings

**Team Member App:**
- View upcoming schedule (syncs to phone calendar)
- Request time off, set availability preferences
- Pick up open shifts, initiate shift swaps
- Clock in/out (with geofencing)
- View pay stubs and earned wages
