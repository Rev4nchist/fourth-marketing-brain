# Fourth Value Propositions by Role

## Executive Summary

Different stakeholders in a restaurant or hospitality organization care about different outcomes. This document maps Fourth's value to each key buyer persona.

---

## CFO / Chief Financial Officer

### Primary Concerns
- Labor cost as % of revenue (typically 25-35% in restaurants)
- Payroll accuracy and compliance
- Audit and legal risk exposure
- Predictable technology costs

### Fourth's Value

**Quantified Impact:**
- **2-4% labor cost reduction** through AI-optimized scheduling aligned to demand
- **50% reduction** in payroll errors through automated calculations (tips, overtime, split shifts)
- **$50K-$200K annual savings** in compliance penalty avoidance per 100 locations
- **3-5 vendors consolidated** into one platform, reducing total technology spend

**Key Proof Points:**
- National QSR chain (2,500 locations): Reduced labor costs by 3.2% in first year, saving $12M annually
- Regional casual dining (180 locations): Eliminated $340K in compliance penalties within 6 months
- Fast casual group (400 locations): Consolidated 6 vendors to 1, saving $800K in annual software costs

**Discovery Questions:**
1. "What's your current labor cost as a percentage of revenue?"
2. "How many payroll corrections do you process per pay period?"
3. "Have you had any scheduling compliance penalties in the past 2 years?"
4. "How many workforce management vendors are you paying today?"

### Primary Concerns
- Turnover rate (industry average: 75-150% annually)
- Time-to-hire and recruiting pipeline
- Employee engagement and satisfaction
- Onboarding quality and speed

### Fourth's Value

**Quantified Impact:**
- **15-20% reduction** in hourly employee turnover through better scheduling flexibility
- **40% faster** time-to-hire with integrated ATS and digital onboarding
- **Day-one readiness**: New hires complete 100% of paperwork before first shift
- **2x improvement** in employee satisfaction scores via mobile schedule transparency

**Key Proof Points:**
- Casual dining chain (300 locations): Reduced hourly turnover from 140% to 112% in first year
- Fast casual (200 locations): Cut time-to-hire from 14 days to 8 days
- QSR franchisor (1,200 locations): Achieved 95% onboarding completion rate (up from 60%)

**Discovery Questions:**
1. "What's your current hourly turnover rate?"
2. "How long does it take from application to first shift?"
3. "How much does turnover cost you per employee?"

## CEO / President

### Primary Concerns
- Business growth and scalability
- Operational efficiency and margin improvement
- Risk management
- Competitive advantage

**Executive Summary:**
Fourth is the operating system for your workforce. We help you grow profitably by optimizing your largest controllable expense (labor), eliminating compliance risk, and giving your team the tools to recruit, retain, and manage talent at scale.

**The Fourth Effect:**
- **Protect margins** as you grow: AI scheduling maintains labor targets regardless of location count
- **Scale confidently**: Platform handles 5 to 5,000 locations without architecture changes
- **Reduce risk**: Automated compliance eliminates the #1 source of labor law penalties
- **Win talent**: Modern mobile experience attracts and retains the hourly workforce your competitors are fighting for
