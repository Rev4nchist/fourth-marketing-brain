# Fourth Workforce Management

**Workforce Management unifies scheduling and time tracking in one intelligent platform---helping operators align labor with demand, control costs, and improve team performance.**

## Overview

Workforce Management connects scheduling, time tracking, tip management, and compliance in one connected platform --- and seamlessly shares data with your chosen payroll system. Powered by Fourth iQ, it uses AI forecasting to predict labor needs, recommend shift adjustments, and help managers make faster, data-driven decisions that protect service quality and margins. It gives leaders full visibility into labor performance while improving consistency and engagement across teams. With less time spent on manual tasks, operators can focus on service, profitability, and growth.

## Value Propositions

- **Plan with precision: Uses AI-powered labor forecasting to schedule the right people at the right time.**
- **Reduce complexity: Simplifies scheduling, compliance, and payroll processes in a single connected workflow.**
- **Improve engagement: Empowers managers and employees with visibility, communication, and predictability.**
- **Control costs: Tracks performance in real time to manage labor spend and maintain profitability across every location.**

## Business Value

- 30% increase in retention
- improved forecast accuracy across every market.

## Customer Evidence

### Time & Attendance

**Time & Attendance helps restaurants track hours, breaks, and overtime accurately without manual fixes or compliance headaches. It connects directly to your POS, so every hour worked is paid correctly and every rule is applied automatically.**

Gives managers real-time visibility into who's on the clock, where they're working, and whether they're in compliance with labor laws. Employees can clock in through your chosen POS, while automated rules help prevent missed punches or costly errors. With one view of all time and attendance data, you can ensure payroll runs accurately and confidently every time.

**Value Propositions:**

- **Track time automatically: Every punch syncs directly from your POS or mobile app for real-time accuracy.**
- **Protect compliance: Apply labor laws, breaks, and overtime rules automatically across locations.**
- **Save hours on payroll prep: Eliminate manual edits, timecard corrections, and double entry.**
- **Build trust: Ensure every employee is paid correctly and managers can see it all in real time.**

**Business Value:** Reduced time-tracking errors by up to 90%; faster payroll processing and reconciliation; fewer compliance violations and disputes; improved accuracy and employee trust.
