# Fourth iQ AI Platform

Fourth iQ is the AI engine built into every aspect of the Fourth Platform. It operates at two levels: in-store operations and above-store strategy.

## Fourth iQ for In-Store Operations

**Fourth iQ is the AI engine built into every aspect of the Fourth Platform. The capabilities for in-store operations predicts demand, recommends next-best actions, and helps managers make faster, more confident decisions that improve performance and profitability.**

Fourth iQ is key to supercharging in-store performance using the Fourth Platform. Directly connected to both Workforce Management and Inventory Management, it analyzes live operational data to forecast demand, identify risks, and recommend the best actions in real time. Highly granular forecasting removes the guesswork for managers and forms the cornerstone of Fourth's Labor and Inventory Optimisation solutions.  The iQ App recommends the 'next best actions' to managers to drive fast and proactive decsions - whether adjusting schedules, inventory, or prep levels. Fourth iQ continually learns from every transaction and decision, helping restaurant teams reduce waste, optimize resources, and improve profitability shift after shift.

### Value Propositions

- **Predict demand with confidence: Delivers accurate forecasts for labor, sales, and inventory using live operational data.**
- **Guide smarter actions: Recommends the next best move for managers and teams directly in their workflow.**
- **Act in real time: Sends alerts and actions through the iQ App so operators can respond instantly when conditions change.**
- **Improve every shift: Learns from past data and continuously refines recommendations to drive better results over time.**

**Business Value:** Up to 10% improvement in forecast accuracy; 25% reduction in manual adjustments; measurable improvement in labor efficiency and margin performance.

## Fourth iQ for Above-Store Strategy

**Fourth iQ is the AI engine built into every aspect of the Fourth Platform. The capabilities for above-store strategy provides senior leaders with complete visbility of how each location is performing and guides them to define a winning strategy that translates  into in-store action.  Think of it as a performance operating system for your entire business.**

Fourth iQ connects forecasting, above-store control, and in-store execution to create consistent, measurable performance across every location. It integrates data from labor, inventory, POS, and financial systems to give leadership teams clearer visibility into performance and greater ability to influence results.  Using AI and automation, Fourth iQ's above-store capabilies tracks KPIs, identifies correlations and anomalies, predicts what is likely to happen next, and triggers the right in-store actions before issues arise. It gives above-store leaders the visibility to make faster, better decisions cascade down to managers in every location. Fourth iQ transforms the Fourth Platform from set of workflow solutions into an always-on performance engine.

### Value Propositions

- **Connect every system: Integrate data from POS, payroll, inventory, and HR into a single, connected view of business performance.**
- **Ensure maximun ROI from tech invesment: Understand how effectively each location is utilising their tech and identify where action is required to increase adoption and effectiveness**
- **Identify the patterns that power performance: Give senior and regional leaders an understanding of the anomalies and correlations across operational metrics that indicate where action is required to drive performance improvements.**
- **Strengthen every decision: Turn operational noise into clear, actionable insights that guide smarter, faster action across the enterprise.**
